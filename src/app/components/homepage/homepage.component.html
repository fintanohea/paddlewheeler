THE PADDLEWHEELER PUB
The paddlewheeler pub is a new westminster icon. The paddlewheeler opened as part of the original westminster quay public market back in 1986. Today, the paddlewheeler is best known for it’s award winning patio on the boardwalk along the beautiful fraser river. A true sports pub with it’s 11 hd tv’s and 3 hd big screens. The 15 taps behind the bar have something for everyone’s taste buds, because the paddlewheeler is a true supporter of locally brewed craft beers. If food is what you’re looking for, we have pub favourites from saucy chicken wings to juicy burgers, fresh seafood, salad’s and daily homemade soups. So on your next stroll down the boardwalk, be sure to stop in for a visit, you won’t be disappointed.
